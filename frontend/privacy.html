🧩 Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Daily Code Snippet ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • Your Google account ID

We do not collect your name, photo, phone number, or any other personal data.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To save your code snippets and favorites
  • To authenticate you securely via JWT tokens

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Render. We do not sell, share, or distribute your data to third parties.

Third-Party Services

  • Google OAuth — for authentication only
  • Google Gemini AI — for generating code snippets (no personal data is sent)
  • Render — backend and database hosting
  • Vercel — frontend hosting

Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to store your authentication token. No tracking cookies are used.

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email below.

Contact

For any privacy concerns, contact: shagunkimothi@gmail.com

Changes to This Policy
